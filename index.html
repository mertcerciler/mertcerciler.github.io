Toproffer

Toproffer is a mobile application where you become aware of the campaigns of the restaurants in anytime from anywhere! By its user friendly User Interface, it is easy to seek around the map and see what kind of attractive campaigns that restaurants have. You can easily filter the campaigns in the map for your precious desires. Dont forget to follow your favourite restaurants to get notified of their instant campaigns, because you will have very limited time to utilize it! Dont miss this opportunity where you can easily save your money in your favourite restaurants at your best moments.
